    + [原始宽度](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/picture/originalwidth/)获取图片的原始宽度，以像素为单位。
    + [原始高度](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/picture/originalheight/)获取图片的原始高度，单位为像素。
